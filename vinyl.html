Vinyl

Vinyl is a general name for a (flexible and resilient) plastic floor covering made of hard material. It is sometimes better known as Novilon. Sometimes vinyl is confused with linoleum, certainly not because there are certainly differences. You can read this for yourself on this website. Vinyl is a synthetic material, while linoleum is pure natural. Because of the natural character of linoleum, it is less resistant to water. If the linoleum floor is too wet, this material will infuse blisters, which is what with vinyl is not the case.

Synthetic floors can be found everywhere: living room, bedroom, but also in hospitals or offices and shops, ... They are easy to install, maintenance-friendly and good for the budget. it is not surprising that this type of floor is popular.

Vinyl is available in thicknesses from 1 to 3.5 millimeters and in widths from 1 to 5 meters The general rule is: The more the floor is loaded, the thick stronger and stronger the quality of the vinyl must be.

Types of vinyl floors, in fact there are too many to list them all.
